Frequently Asked Questions



Why should I complete the test?

Our online test not only helps you understand your skin type, but also gives you product recommendations from professionals. We also offer products suited for many specific skin types (with discounts!)

Do I have to sign up to complete the test?

No, to find out your skin type you do not need to enter your email anywhere. Once you have completed your test, you can choose to sign up and then receive further recommendations tailored for you.


How do you know what products are best for me?

We use the questionnaire to understand basic information about your skin type. We consult with professional dermatologists to determine the best quality products for every specific customer, and we make sure to include products of all price ranges.

Where do you ship?

We are a Latvia-based small business, so our products are available in all of Europe (with free shipping!) and North America. We are trying to expand soon, so keep an eye out if Your home country is not on the list yet!
